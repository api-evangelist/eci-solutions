# ECI Solutions (eci-solutions)

ECI Solutions is a leading provider of cloud-based business management software for small and medium-sized businesses across manufacturing, distribution, building and construction, field service, and retail. ECI's integrated suites span ERP, e-commerce, CRM, accounting, inventory, and analytics, helping customers streamline operations and grow.

### ECI Solutions Platform API

The ECI Solutions platform exposes integration points across its ERP and business management suites (including JobBOSS2, M1, Macola, DDMSPLUS, RockSolid, Bolt, and others) to connect with third-party e-commerce, CRM, accounting, and field-service tools. Public OpenAPI documentation is not currently available; integrations are typically arranged via the relevant product support channel or partner program.
